## Why This Matrix Looks the Way It Does

The book explicitly states, in its Introduction, that it does **not** teach market structure/trend analysis as a standalone topic: "This book does not teach market analysis in detail because market analysis is a complete topic on its own... we assume that everyone using this book already understands how to analyze the market technically and fundamentally" (see `knowledge/SOURCE_INDEX.md`, "Before You Use POIs, Know This"). That is why almost every row above is "Partially defined" at best — the book uses trend/structure vocabulary constantly as *context* for its POI and BTMM rules, but deliberately does not supply a standalone trend-confirmation or market-structure-mapping methodology. This matches the gap already recorded in `docs/PROJECT_STATE.md` Phase 0A section 6 ("Missing Definitions").

- Swing detection update (Ambiguity 10, resolved provisionally): a Meaningful Swing High and Swing Low Detection Standard Version 1 — Provisional now exists (`knowledge/MEASUREMENT_STANDARDS.md`), covering the local pivot window, plateau handling, and meaningful-reversal confirmation. Higher High, Higher Low, Lower High, Lower Low, Break of Structure, and Change of Character all **remain undefined**, and no market-structure sequence rules were added by this decision.
- Trendline construction/touches update (Ambiguity 11, resolved provisionally): a Bullish and Bearish Trendline Detection and Validation Standard Version 1 — Provisional now exists (`knowledge/MEASUREMENT_STANDARDS.md`), covering eligible swing anchors, minimum anchor spacing, the slope equation, Normalized-Slope steepness rejection, touch/pierce tolerances, and draft/confirmed/strong progression. Final trendline invalidation, retest, reclaim, false break, sweep, and expiration all **remain undefined**.
- Support/Resistance update (Ambiguity 12, resolved provisionally): a Support and Resistance Detection and Validation Standard Version 1 — Provisional now exists (`knowledge/MEASUREMENT_STANDARDS.md`), covering eligible meaningful-swing origins, the initial-creator principle (fixed, non-repainting boundaries), origin-reaction gating, touch/pierce tolerances, distinct-touch confirmation, and draft/confirmed/strong progression. This standard is explicitly **Author-Approved, Engineering-Provisional, NOT YET empirically calibrated or out-of-sample validated, and NOT production-approved**. Final invalidation, confirmed break, reclaim, retest, false break, role reversal, freshness, mitigation, expiration, zone merging, and zone ranking all **remain undefined**. Equal Highs/Equal Lows and Trendline formulas were not modified by this decision.
- Break of Structure and Change of Character: recorded for completeness only; **not adopted** as project rules.
