## Book definition

A diagonal form of resistance where price has reacted at least twice; a bearish trendline can act as a selling POI.

## Required market location

Diagonal, connecting swing highs within a downtrend.

## Formation conditions

At least two touches to establish the line.

## Confirmation conditions

The third or fourth touch can create a sell opportunity.

## Origin candle or level
