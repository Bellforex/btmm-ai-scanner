## Book definition

A three-candle reversal pattern at supply/resistance: strong bullish candle, then a small/doji candle, then a strong bearish candle - a full volume switch from buyers to sellers.

## Required market location

Around a supply zone or resistance structure.

## Formation conditions

Candle 1: strong bullish. Candle 2: small candle or doji. Candle 3: strong bearish.

## Confirmation conditions

The final (third) candle must be very strong, ideally at least 2 to 3 times the size of the middle doji candle.

## Origin candle or level

The middle doji candle's area is explicitly called out as the POI itself.
