# Hammer

## Alternative book name

The book explicitly states "the Hammer or Pin Bar" (paragraph 477) - Pin Bar is the same pattern under a second name, not a separate POI.

## Family

Price Action POI

## Direction

Bullish

## Source pages

"2. Hammer - Shooting star" (paragraphs 475-496).

## Book definition

A single-candle rejection pattern with a long lower wick and small body, appearing at a support zone: sellers pushed price down, buyers rejected it strongly back up.

## Required market location

Around a support zone.

## Formation conditions

Long lower wick, small body.

## Confirmation conditions

If the Hammer closes bullish (green), it is a stronger standalone buy signal. If it closes bearish (red), the book explicitly says do not buy immediately - wait for a bullish confirmation candle.

## Origin candle or level

The single Hammer candle.

## Upper boundary

Not explicitly given as a formal zone formula (presumably the candle's own high, but the book does not state this explicitly for this POI).

## Lower boundary

Not explicitly given as a formal zone formula.

## Wick treatment

The long lower wick is the primary rejection signal.

## Body treatment

Body must be "small" - no ratio given. This is conceptually the same open question as Ambiguity 6 (registered for Pressure Wick) but is not itself part of that registered ambiguity's scope; recorded here as an analogous, separately-open question for this POI.

## Candle-size requirement

Not defined as a 2x/3x multi-candle ratio (this POI's validity rule is about single-candle wick:body shape and close color, not a candle-to-candle size comparison).

## Volume or momentum proxy
