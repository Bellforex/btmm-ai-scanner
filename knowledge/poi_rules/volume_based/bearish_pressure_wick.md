# Bearish Pressure Wick

## Alternative book name

"Pressure wick" / "liquidity wick" used interchangeably (paragraphs 359, 369). Same POI, two names.

## Family

Volume-Based POI

## Direction

Bearish (acts as a future selling zone when price returns to it).

## Source pages

"5. Pressure wick" (paragraphs 359-373).

## Book definition

A strong rejection wick where price pushes up into a zone, collects liquidity, and is strongly rejected downward, while the candle still closes with a body that has "good proportion."

## Required market location

Not location-restricted beyond appearing where price has pushed into a zone and been rejected; best identified on 2H/3H/4H and above.

## Formation conditions

A long upper rejection wick combined with a meaningful candle body.

## Confirmation conditions

Drop to a lower timeframe for confirmation and precise entry once price returns to the wick - do not enter blindly.

## Origin candle or level

The single wick candle.

## Upper boundary

Not explicitly given as a drawing formula - recorded as a gap (see Bullish Pressure Wick).

## Lower boundary

Not explicitly given as a drawing formula.

## Wick treatment

The long wick is the core signal, exempted from the standard displacement/size-ratio rule per Measurement Standard V1 SS7.

## Body treatment

"Good proportion" required - unquantified (Ambiguity 6, unresolved).

## Candle-size requirement
